About Me

Hi I'm Les Bro!

    Gather 'round young adventurers to hear of a quiet elf that prefered her time to be spent in the worlds of her head. After some talk with her Chieftess (hi Mom) she picked up the quill (or keyboard) and released her ideas to the world, becoming the Skald she was destined to become.
    Her main goal is to help people in their journey to great stories, fantastical escape, and unforgetable memories with their closest friends.
    On this website, you'll find a list of all the modules, races, classes, characters, and more that Les has worked on in the past couple years. Feel free to use the items I've posted to DM's Guild in your own games, and if you do, I'm honored to be a part of the memories you're about to make!
